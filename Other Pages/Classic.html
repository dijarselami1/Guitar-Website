    The Acustic guitar

    An acoustic guitar is a musical instrument in the string family. When a string is plucked its vibration is transmitted from the bridge, resonating throughout the top of the guitar. It is also transmitted to the side and back of the instrument, resonating through the air in the body, and producing sound from the sound hole.The acustic guitar is mostly used to strum chords, because of its beautiful and metalic sound most of the songs have acustic covers.
