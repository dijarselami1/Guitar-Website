    The Guitar as an instrument

    The guitar is the second most famous instrument in the world. With its 6 strings and simple looking body its one of the hardest instruments to learn. It is the instrument of many legendary guitarists like Jimi Hendrix
    , Jimmy Page
    and so on. Its history goes back as far as the 1500BC in Egypt, an guitar-like intrument called the "Tanbur". While the first Modern guitar was made by Antonio de Torres in the mid 1800's.
